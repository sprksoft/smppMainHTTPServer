How to install Smartschool++

1

Go to the Chrome Webstore

Go to the Webstore and look for Smartschool++, or Lite

2

Click "Install"

Click & then allow all the required permissions

3

Go to your Smartschool

Go to your Smartschool & refresh the page. Congratulation, your journey just started!

How to use Widgets

1

Enter edit mode

Click the edit button
at the top left to enter

2

Open the drawer

Open the drawer at the top to grab some widgets

3

Drop the widgets

Place the widgets in existing panels or create new ones

4

Stow them away

Store your widgets by dropping them back in the drawer

How to use Quick Menu

1

Open Quick with :

Open the quick menu using
the : key

2

Type it out!

Look up any function in quick menu, for example "planner"

3

Navigate the menu

Use Tab to navigate and Enter to confirm your choice

4

Save your own Quicks

Use "quick add" to save a shortcut. Enter a name: (eg. google), then a value (URL): (e.g. https://google.com)
